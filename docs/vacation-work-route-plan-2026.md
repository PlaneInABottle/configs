# 2026 Vacation-Work Route Plan

Date prepared: 2026-07-05

## Constraints

- Vacation balance: 13 days.
- Office days: Monday and Tuesday.
- Remote work possible: Wednesday through Friday.
- Preferred trip pattern A: take Tuesday off, fly Tuesday, work remotely Wednesday-Friday, return Saturday or Sunday. Uses 1 vacation day.
- Preferred trip pattern B: take Tuesday off, then take the next Monday and Tuesday off. This creates roughly a 10-day trip and uses 3 vacation days.
- Not preferred right now: fly after Tuesday office hours for nearby routes.
- Exclude: Europe, Canada, USA, Russia, short-distance countries.
- Do not recommend: Japan, Malaysia, Thailand, Argentina, Singapore, Bali.
- Prefer destinations with Turkish Airlines service from Istanbul, then use ZED for local or regional add-ons.

## Best Allocation

Recommended use of 13 days:

- 3 long trips x 3 days = 9 vacation days.
- 4 short-style remote-work trips x 1 day = 4 vacation days.

This gives seven trips total while preserving the user's preferred Tuesday departure structure.

## Primary Recommendations

### 1. Mexico City + Bogota

Use as a long trip.

Why:

- Turkish Airlines flies IST -> MEX daily in July 2026 sample data.
- FlightConnections sample: TK184/TK181/TK182, 01:50 IST -> 07:05 MEX, 14h15.
- Tuesday departure works well because arrival is Tuesday morning local time.
- ZED spot check MEX -> BOG on 2026-09-13:
  - 46 total flights.
  - 43 selectable flights.
  - 7 one-leg selectable flights after max-leg filtering.
  - Examples: AM717 00:20-06:05, AV187 00:40-06:15, AM761 08:50-14:40, AV073 14:00-19:35.
  - Price sample for AM717: 7235.63 TRY preferred total.

Recommendation:

- Make Mexico City the long anchor.
- Use Bogota as the ZED add-on rather than Venezuela.
- If doing a 10-day version, spend Tue-Sun in Mexico City and then Sun/Tue-Wed in Bogota, or reverse depending on return inventory.

### 2. Cape Town + Mauritius

Use as a long trip if pairing with Mauritius, or as a short-style remote-work trip if only Cape Town.

Why:

- Turkish Airlines flies IST -> CPT daily in July 2026 sample data.
- FlightConnections sample: TK44, 01:45 IST -> 11:50 CPT, 11h05.
- The Tuesday arrival is workable for remote work from Wednesday.
- ZED spot check CPT -> MRU on 2026-09-13:
  - 8 total flights.
  - 1 selectable one-leg flight.
  - MK844 23:35 CPT -> 06:40 MRU, MID chance.
  - Price sample: 6275.49 TRY preferred total.
- Turkish Airlines also serves Mauritius, so MRU works as either the add-on or a backup direct TK endpoint.

Important caveat:

- CPT -> JNB domestic ZED sample on 2026-09-13 had 52 total flights but 0 selectable. South African Airways results showed "Staff category not allowed"; other airlines had no ID agreement. Do not build the plan around domestic South Africa ZED unless rechecked on exact dates.

Recommendation:

- If Cape Town is only Cape Town: short-style trip is possible but tight.
- If Cape Town + Mauritius: use the 3-day long pattern.

### 3. Bogota + optional Cartagena/Caribbean by paid/ZED recheck

### 4. Seoul or Taipei

## Venezuela / Caracas

Operationally possible but not recommended as a priority.

Evidence:

- Turkish Airlines flies IST -> CCS several times per week in July 2026 sample data.
- MEX -> CCS ZED direct one-leg sample on 2026-09-13 had 0 returned one-leg selectable flights.
- MEX -> CCS with max 2 legs had many selectable results, but all sampled results were LOW chance and routed via PTY, BOG, IAH, or MIA.
- BOG -> CCS on 2026-09-16 had 3 selectable one-leg options: LA4404, AV142, AV122.

Risk:

- U.S. State Department advisory issued 2026-06-27 lists Venezuela as Level 3, reconsider travel, with some Level 4 do-not-travel areas.
- UK FCDO page current on 2026-07-05 advises against all travel to parts of Venezuela and all but essential travel to remaining areas; it also notes limited commercial flight availability and temporary closure of Maiquetia airport to commercial flights.

Recommendation:

- Do not combine Mexico with Venezuela as the default plan.
- If Venezuela remains a personal priority, route through Bogota and recheck official advisories, airport operations, insurance, and exact ZED inventory close to travel.
